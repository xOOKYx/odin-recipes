Amazing Pan Fried Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 1 Bunch - Asparagus
  • 1 Tbsp - Olive Oil
  • 1/4 Cup - Butter
  • 2 Tsp - Pepper
  • 1 Tsp - Salt

Steps

  • Heat a cast iron pan over medium high heat
  • Lightly oil the pan
  • Break asparagus by bending it
  • Wash asparagus
  • Place in a bowl and cover with oil
  • Season asparagus with salt and pepper
  • Mix well to cover evenly
  • Add asparagus to the pan
  • Cook until tender
  • Add butter and cook for 2-3 mins
  • Serve with your favorite dish
