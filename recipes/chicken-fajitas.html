The Best Chicken Fajitas!

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs - Chicken Thighs
  • 1 - Onion
  • 2 - Bell Peppers
  • 3 - Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 tbsp - Paprika
  • 1 tbsp - Pepper
  • 2 tsp - Salt
  • 1 tsp - Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp - Garlic Powder

Steps:

  • Heat cast iron pan over medium-high heat
  • Add oil to the pan
  • Allow oil to heat
  • Cut chicken into strips
  • Chop onions and bell peppers
  • Dice garlic
  • Place chicken in the pan and season with all spices
  • Cook chicken by flipping occasionally
  • Once chicken is cooked add in onions, bell peppers, and garlic
  • Allow vegetables to cook for 10 minutes
  • Stir occasionally
  • Serve on a warmed tortilla
