The Best Homemade Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups - Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Cup - Shredded Parmesean Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup - Butter
  • 1 Tbsp - Olive Oil
  • 1/2 - Diced Shallot
  • 2 - Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp - Italion Seasoning

Steps:

  • Prep all ingredients
  • Heat a medium sauce pan over medium heat
  • Add 1 Tbsp of oil to the man and allow to heat for 30 sec
  • Add shallots and cook till clear
  • Add garlic and cook till aromatic
  • Add Seasoning
  • Pour in heavy whipping cream slowly
  • Add in butter
  • Strick contiously until it begins to thicken
  • Add parmesean cheese
  • Stir until melted and thickened
